## 📖 About

**Brush & Soul** is a modern, elegant, and fully responsive website designed for a professional painter. The site serves as a digital gallery, online store, and personal brand hub — allowing art lovers to explore, connect, and purchase original paintings.

### ✨ Key Features

- 🖼️ **Art Gallery** — Filterable portfolio with lightbox viewing
- 🛒 **Online Shop** — Browse and purchase original paintings & prints
- 👩‍🎨 **About the Artist** — Biography, journey, and artistic statement
- 📬 **Contact & Commissions** — Inquiry form for custom artwork
- 📱 **Fully Responsive** — Looks stunning on all devices
- ⚡ **Fast & Lightweight** — Optimized images and clean code
- 🎨 **Elegant Design** — Minimal layout that lets the art shine
- 📸 **Instagram Integration** — Showcases latest posts from social media
- 💌 **Newsletter Signup** — Stay updated on new works and exhibitions
- 💬 **Testimonials** — Reviews from art collectors and clients
