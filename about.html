Mbanase Nyiri

Professional Painter & Visual Artist

Mbanase Nyiri is a contemporary visual artist whose work spans across figurative art, landscapes, and ink drawings. Her bold artistic vision brings together the beauty of the human form, the richness of African landscapes, and the raw emotion of everyday life.

From striking chalk figures on dark canvases to vibrant African sunsets and delicate ink illustrations, her work reflects a deep connection to identity, culture, and the natural world. Each piece tells a unique story, inviting viewers to pause and connect with something deeper within themselves.

Working primarily with acrylics, chalk, ink, and mixed media, Mbanase creates art that is both visually stunning and emotionally powerful. Her paintings can be found in private collections and have been exhibited in various galleries and art shows.

Artist Statement

"Art is my language — the way I speak to the world when words fall short. Every stroke on my canvas carries a piece of my story, my heritage, and my vision of the world as I see it.

I am drawn to the human form — the way bodies move, express, and hold untold stories. I am equally captivated by the landscapes of Africa — the golden sunsets, the vast savannas, the quiet power of nature.

Whether I'm working with chalk on a dark canvas, painting a vivid landscape, or creating delicate ink drawings, my goal is always the same: to create art that makes you feel something. Art that stays with you. Art that speaks to your soul."

— Mbanase Nyiri

My Journey

The milestones that shaped my artistic path

2020

The Beginning

Discovered a deep passion for visual art. Started experimenting with different mediums — chalk, ink, and acrylics.

2021

Finding My Style

Began focusing on figurative art and African landscapes. Bold chalk on dark canvases became a signature technique.

2022

First Exhibition

Showcased original paintings at a local gallery. Sold first pieces to private collectors.

2023

Growing Community

Built a strong following on Instagram. Started taking commissions and expanding into ink drawings and mixed media.

2024

Brush & Soul

Launched this website to share art with the world. New collections and exhibitions on the horizon.
